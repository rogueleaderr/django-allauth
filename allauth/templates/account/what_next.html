Now let's talk a little bit about how LinerNotes works. You can think of LinerNotes as as a "home base" for online muisc. Come here and we'll satisfy your every music need (or point you to someone who can.)

LinerNotes is made up of a bunch of "baseball cards" that have all the essential facts about artists, albums, and songs. Like baseball cards, you can either read the cards to learn about a band, or you can grab your favorites and build a collection.

Why? Collections help you remember your favorite stuff, find it again quickly, and share it with your friends easily.

Plus, once you make a collection we'll make you a sweet custom playlist for everything in that collection.
